About

Our website, ArtWalli, is an interactive
website where users can either draw,
design, or download thier very own
wallpapers.

In the drawing section, users are allowed to use
our digital brush and canvas to create whatever
design they please. They may save this to their device and
use it as their new wallpaper. For the design section,
users have the ability to customize some of our pre-made
designs to add a personal touch to their wallpaper.
Similar to the drawing section, they may save their creation
after to use as a wallpaper. Lastly, in the download section,
users may choose from a number of premade wallpapers. They can
download these images for free and use it as a wallpaper.
This is not a created webpage but instead a pinterest board
made by yours truly. These wallpapers are available for
phones, tablets, and desktops.
